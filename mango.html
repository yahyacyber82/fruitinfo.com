Fruit Info

Mango:

About a Mango:

  • The mango is a delicious tropical fruit known as the "King of Fruits" because it is loved by people all over the world.
  • Mangoes can be yellow, orange, red, or green, often with a beautiful mix of colors on a single fruit.
  • They have a creamy, buttery texture and a very sweet, exotic flavor that feels like a treat.
  • In the center, there is one large, flat pit (seed) that is surrounded by juicy, golden-yellow flesh.
  • Mangoes are packed with energy and nutrients that help you stay active and healthy.

Benefits:

  • Eye Health: Because mangoes are loaded with Vitamin A, they help you see better, especially in the dark.
  • Glowing Skin: The high vitamin content helps keep your skin clear, soft, and glowing from the inside out.
  • Immune Booster: With so much Vitamin C, mangoes act like a natural medicine to help your body fight off colds.
  • Digestion Helper: Mangoes contain unique enzymes that help break down proteins, making them very easy on your stomach.

Nutrition Factor:

  • Calories: A whole mango has about 202 calories, making it a more filling and substantial snack than an apple.
  • Vitamin A: Provides over 60% of your Daily Value. This is essential for maintaining clear eyesight and healthy skin.
  • High Vitamin C: Supplies about 67% of your daily needs, which is even more than some oranges! It helps your body heal and stay strong.
  • Fiber: Contains about 5 grams of fiber, which keeps your digestion moving smoothly and helps you feel full.
  • Vitamin B6: Offers about 11% of your daily needs, supporting brain health and giving you a natural mood boost.
  • Antioxidants: Rich in compounds like mangiferin, which help protect your heart and cells from damage.
  • Fat & Sodium Free: Like our other fruits, mangoes are naturally free of fat and sodium.

Fun Facts:

  • Ancient History: People have been growing and eating mangoes for over 4,000 years, starting in India.
  • Family Ties: Surprisingly, mangoes are related to cashews and pistachios!
  • Symbol of Love: In many cultures, giving someone a basket of mangoes is considered a gesture of friendship and love.
  • The World's Favorite: More fresh mangoes are eaten every day than any other fruit in the world!
